We are glad to introduce ourselves as 'Bharat Darshan Tours and Travels' a india tour package and car rental service provider company which was founded on the principal of providing personalized service to its customers and making use of its vast experience in the travel industry. It is our aim to supply a complete co-ordination of the services and punctuality in departure and arrival at the destinations, by means of accurate programming. We are serving our customers since 1989 with our fleets and tour packages, our goal is to provide best service by our car rental and packages, but it is understood like a personalized service adapted to the customer needs, with dedicated staff, and a different proposal for different demands.
